# Product Order Process (Food & Physical Goods)

We have streamlined our order process to ensure transparency and ease for both food and physical goods. Here is how it works:

**Summary:** Browse → Select → Pay → Track → Receive

## 1. Product Discovery
Browse our catalog to find the food or products you need.

## 2. Product Selection
Select your desired items, specifying:
- **Quantity**
- **Size / Dimensions**
- **Variation (e.g., flavor, color)**

## 3. Add to Cart / Place Order
Add items to your cart and proceed to checkout.

## 4. Price Confirmation
Review the total cost, including:
- Item prices
- Delivery fees
- Taxes

## 5. Payment
Securely pay for your order using your preferred method.

## 6. Order Received
The system acknowledges your order immediately. You will receive a notification that your order has been placed.

## 7. Provider Confirmation
The provider checks availability and confirms your order.
- **Status Update:** *Order Confirmed*

## 8. Processing & Fulfillment
The provider prepares your order.
- **Status Update:** *Processing / Preparing*

## 9. On the Way
Your order is out for delivery.
- **Status Update:** *On the Way*

## 10. Delivery & Receipt
The order is delivered to your location.
- **Status Update:** *Delivered*
- You mark the order as **Received** to complete the process.

## 11. Customer Support
Rate your experience or contact support if you have any issues.
